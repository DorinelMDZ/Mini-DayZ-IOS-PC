## Survival

**Health System:**

- When health is below 50, blood cannot regenerate.
- When health reaches 0, health will continuously decrease.

**Poisoning vs Illness:**

- Eating raw meat at 0°C may increase the risk of illness.
- Without protective measures, stepping on acid, entering a contaminated area, or entering a biological container can lead to poisoning.
- Illness: Accelerates consumption of water and food, continuously reduces health.
- Poisoning: Continuously depletes health and reduces health points.
- When sick or poisoned, blood cannot return naturally.

When satiety, hydration, and body temperature are all at 80%+ and health is at 50%+, there is a higher probability of treating illness and poisoning.

**Wetness:** Getting wet for more than 10 seconds in rain without a raincoat marks you as wet. Temperature -2 and movement speed reduced. Removes after 30 seconds out of rain or 10 seconds near a hot pot.

**Darkness:** Starts getting dark at 7 PM; completely dark at midnight. In Legendary difficulty, the bunker is completely dark.

**Contamination:** Entering a contaminated area without a gas mask causes continuous health decrease, followed by poisoning after 5 seconds.

**Radiation:** Starting from Island 5, radiation exists around and inside bunkers. Entering without protective clothing causes significant health drop, followed by bleeding after 10 seconds.

**New Island - Polluted Island:** All military camps are contaminated areas and rain turns to acid rain.

**Other:**

- Temperatures drop drastically on Snow Island in Legendary difficulty.
- It only snows on Snow Island; other islands only get rain.
- You can click to pick up and recycle placed barbed wire, bear traps, Claymore mines, item hiding places, and generators.
- A new “Scavenge” button allows looting helmeted/poison-resistant/protective suit zombies (reduces your health).
- Killing bandits now has a chance to drop epoxy putty, tape, and cleaning kits.
- Defeating Gorka bandits drops the Black Gorka Helmet.
- Equipment and items can be stored on the vehicle.
- Vehicle fuel consumption is halved.
- Vehicles can be reinforced with blowtorches and metal plates, making them immune to collision damage.
- Teammates can cross islands and enter/exit the bunker with you.
- Barbed wire can damage enemies.
- Remove the tent’s monster-luring mechanism.

## World Rules

### Positive Rules

- **【Immortal】** — Equipment, weapons, and vehicles will not be damaged.
- **【Dimensional Backpack】** — Infinite stacking!
- **[Fatty]** — Maximum health increased by half. Upper limits for satiety and water intake are doubled.
- **[Leisure]** — Resurrected on the West Coast after death.

### Negative Rules

- **[Immersive Apocalypse]** — ⚠️ All creature health bars are invisible. ⚠️ The number of infected people has increased significantly.
- **[Mutant]** — ☣ Infected individuals will become stronger every day, reaching maximum strength after 30 days. Maximum health and damage multiplier: 500%.
- **[Bloodshed]** — A blood moon appears every 7 days. Begins at 7 PM and lasts throughout the day; all zombies will chase you. Feeling tired if you don’t sleep at night.
- **[A Hail of Bullets]** — NPCs wielding automatic weapons will fire in rapid succession.
